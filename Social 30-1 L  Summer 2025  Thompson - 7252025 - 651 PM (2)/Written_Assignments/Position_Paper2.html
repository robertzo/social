Writing Assignment - Position Paper (Full Version)

Introduction

In this assignment, you will write a COMPLETE POSITION PAPER; the second of two writing tasks we need for this course.

  • Assignment II requires students to demonstrate the skills of analysis, evaluation, and synthesis in response to a written source. 
  • Students will be presented with a written source that they must analyze to demonstrate an understanding of the perspective(s) reflected in the source.
  • The position paper requires students to argue a position taken in response to a question regarding the perspective(s) reflected in the source. 

This is NOT a research paper or an opinion paper on the good or bad of Liberalism. It is about the Perspective on Liberalism in the Source. A position paper asks us to take a position on a topic, issue, source, or perspective, and back it up with arguments and evidence. Arguments and Evidence are often confused as the same things.  Arguments are the why we should believe you, and evidence is the example of the why. 

Assignment

Analyze the following source and complete the assignment. 

Assignment

Source:

Individuals have a responsibility to participate in the democratic process. Without their active participation, the country would be vulnerable to abuse of power.

Question:

  • To what extent should one embrace the ideological perspective(s) reflected in the source?

Write an ESSAY in which you:

  • analyze the source to demonstrate an understanding of the ideological perspective(s) reflected in the source. 
  • establish and argue a position in response to the question presented
  • support your position and arguments by using evidence from your knowledge and understanding of Social Studies
